After all my years of living and failed relationships I know one thing, that I cannot make you love me anymore
than you already do. The uncomfortable truth is that no amount of gestures, no amount of love letters,
no amount of money will be able to satisify a disatisifed brain. As the brain is selfish and unforgiving,
its cunning and self-destructive. The mind floods itself with "what if's", the endless amount of possibilites
being rapidly fed into your mind will slowly rot you from the inside and soon, it'll rot our love too.
You'll regret ever meeting me, ever being my lover, for ever seeing a future with me. Though youll never
be able to forget me, youll never be able to forget the sound of my voice, the way I speak,, the way I speak of you,
how my hands feel on your body, you wont be able to forget the way I describe my feelings of love and admiration
towards you in each letter. This is one of the most interesting things about the human brain, even if you grow
disatisfied with our relationship, youll never be able to forget the feelings, the passion weve once had for
each other. You'll never be able to forget the laughs and smiles we've shared together.
